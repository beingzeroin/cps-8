Details

  • The contest is open for students of all engineering colleges.
  • There's an open category and four concept specific categories
  • The winners of each category will be awarded prizes
  • The registration fee is Rs.500/-, you can register and then pay the fee.
  • There will be three rounds of competition.
    1. Round 1: MCQ Written Test (Respective College Campus)
    2. Round 2: Qualifying - Online Contest (From Home)
    3. Round 3: Finals - Offline Coding Contest (Being Zero Office)
  • Each participant will recieve a Being Zero T-Shirt and a Certificate of Participation
  • Contact your respective college student ambassadors for further details, no college student ambassador? Register to be one yourself!
  • It's going to be amazing and you are going to love it!
